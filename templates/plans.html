Support

We offer a range of support for those needing it. If you need support due to an issue with your server, you can contact us and we will get to you as soon as possible. If you need support in maintaining your server, we offer support for that as well. If you'd like someone to maintain your server for you, you will need to provide the content, but that will cost an extra $5 a month. Someone will keep your server secure, updated, and upload the content you provide.

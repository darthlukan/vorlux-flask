The Employees Behind Vorlux Hosting

Here is where you'll find who is behind this company and what their roles are.

 • Scott - Head of Server Administration/Linux Administration and Founder:
 Scott is the founder of Vorlux Hosting and some of his responsibilities include maintaining the host server, keeping it up to date with the latest stable  software as well as handling kernel upgrades. He also deals directly with customer service as well as deploying customer servers and making sure that there  is the best service provided.

 • Nathan - Head of Web Content:
 Nathan is head of looking over what content hits the web. With all the changes and editing, he looks over it and helps decide which content needs to be  changed, and which content will hit the main webpages, i.e blog posts, this website, any other web pages in the future. He also deals directly with customer  service and deploying customer servers.

 • Troy - Head of Hardware
 Troy is the head of hardware. He looks for the best hardware to handle the extensive load that the host server will handle as well as troubleshooting and  helping fix hardware issues. He also deals directly with customer service and deploying customers servers and offering help when needed.

 • Stephanie - Head of Public Relations
Stephanie is head of maintaining the social networking aspects, such as our blog, Facebook, and Twitter. She also deals directly with customer service and deploying customers servers.
